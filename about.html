Our People

Lori

Licensed Aesthetician certified in brow arching, microblading, brazilian waxing and dermaplaning. Welcome! My passions lie in great skin care, flawless makeup and beautifully sculpted brows! I'm a proud user of Satin Smooth waxing products, Intensive Tint, LI pigments and Skin Script RX which are easy on even the most sensitive skin types.
